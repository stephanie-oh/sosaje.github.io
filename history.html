The Infiltration of Barbie: A Timeline of Doll Domination

  • The first Barbie doll steps onto the scene, debuting in a black and white striped swimsuit that scandalizes toys everywhere. This marked the beginning of her covert operation to charm the world and establish a base for global doll domination.
  • Barbie didn’t like to mingle with humans alone, so she promptly introduced Ken in 1961, her perfectly coiffed and eternally loyal counterpart, to help manage her expanding empire of style.
  • Barbie gets a makeover, gaining eyes that actually look forward, symbolizing her plans to keep an eye on the future of fashion and world leadership.
  • Barbie becomes an entrepreneur, starting her own companies to fund her secret project: a mind-controlling device cleverly disguised as a hairbrush.
  • Deciding it’s time to influence policy directly, Barbie runs for President, promising a platform of peace, love, and mandatory high-heels.
  • Barbie now dabbles in espionage with the release of "Spy Squad," using her high-tech gadgets and stealth to bug the houses of major world leaders under the guise of innocent playtime.
  • Barbie enters the Metaverse, claiming digital territory to eventually replace world governments with more fashionable, if virtual, leadership.
  • The world wakes up to President Barbie declaring the dawn of a New Pink Order, where every day is fabulous, every law is fashionable, and every dissenter has to wear last season’s shoes.

Remember, in the empire of Barbie, every day is a runway, and loyalty is the trend that never goes out of style.
